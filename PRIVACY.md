# Privacy Policy for Pill Time

**Last Updated:** 04-01-2026

**1. Data Storage**
Pill Time is an offline-first application. All medication logs, schedules, and personal settings are stored locally on your device via SQLite. We do not have servers, and we do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.

**2. AI Chat & Data Transmission**
When you use the AI Health Assistant, your text inputs are sent securely through a proxy server to Google Gemini API.
- We do not store these messages.
- We do not use your data to train models.
- The proxy server exists solely to protect API keys and does not log personal health information.
